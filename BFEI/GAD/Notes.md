# Vertices
Points that compose faces and have a location in space defined by coordinates.
# Faces
Flat surfaces that make the boundaries of a solid shape. Here are always triangles.
# Normals
The direction of a face that determines which side gets rendered. For instance if a normal is at an angle of more than 180 degrees from the viewer it doesn't get rendered. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Normal_(geometry)
# Raster
A 2D picture made by a grid of pixels.
